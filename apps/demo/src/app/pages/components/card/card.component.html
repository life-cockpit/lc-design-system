Card Component

Cards group related content and actions in a visually distinct container. They support multiple variants, customizable padding and border radius, clickable interactions, and selected states.

Variants

Cards come in three visual variants: elevated (box shadow), outlined (border), and flat (no decoration).

Elevated Card
Uses box-shadow for depth. Best for emphasizing content on neutral backgrounds.
Outlined Card
Uses border for definition. Works well in contained layouts or grouped cards.
Flat Card
Minimal styling. Best for subtle content grouping without strong visual separation. 
<lc-card variant="elevated">...</lc-card>
<lc-card variant="outlined">...</lc-card>
<lc-card variant="flat">...</lc-card>

Padding

Control internal spacing with five padding sizes: none, xs, sm, md (default), and lg.

None: 0px padding
Extra Small: 8px padding Small: 12px padding Medium (default): 16px padding Large: 24px padding 
<lc-card padding="none">...</lc-card>
<lc-card padding="xs">...</lc-card>
<lc-card padding="sm">...</lc-card>
<lc-card padding="md">...</lc-card> <!-- default -->
<lc-card padding="lg">...</lc-card>

Border Radius

Customize corner roundness with five border radius options: none, sm, md (default), lg, and full.

None: Sharp corners (0px) Small: Subtle roundness (4px) Medium (default): Balanced (8px) Large: Pronounced (16px) Full: Maximum roundness (9999px) 
<lc-card borderRadius="none">...</lc-card>
<lc-card borderRadius="sm">...</lc-card>
<lc-card borderRadius="md">...</lc-card> <!-- default -->
<lc-card borderRadius="lg">...</lc-card>
<lc-card borderRadius="full">...</lc-card>

Clickable Cards

Cards can be made interactive with the clickable property. Clickable cards show hover effects, focus outlines, and emit click events. They are fully keyboard accessible (Enter/Space keys).

Non-Clickable Card
This card has no interaction. It's used for static content display.
Clickable Card
This card is interactive. Click it or press Enter/Space when focused.
Clicked {{ clickCount() }} times 
<lc-card [clickable]="true" (cardClick)="handleClick()" ariaLabel="Action card">
  ...
</lc-card>

Selected State

Cards can show a selected state with a primary-colored border and background tint. Works well for selectable lists or multi-choice scenarios.

Option 1
Not selected
Option 2
Selected card with primary border
Option 3
Not selected
 
<lc-card [clickable]="true" [selected]="true">...</lc-card>

Title & Subtitle

Use the title and subtitle inputs for consistent card headers without custom classes.

The title is rendered by the card component itself with consistent typography.
Subtitle is useful for additional context or descriptions. 
<lc-card title="Card Title">
  <p>Body content</p>
</lc-card>

<lc-card title="Title" subtitle="Subtitle text">
  <p>Body content</p>
</lc-card>

Content Projection

Cards use ng-content for flexible content projection. Use helper classes .card-header, .card-content, and .card-footer for consistent layout.

Card Header
Main content goes here. This could be text, images, forms, or any other content. The card will automatically adjust its height based on the content. 
<lc-card>
  <div class="card-header">Header</div>
  <div class="card-content">Content</div>
  <div class="card-footer">Footer</div>
</lc-card>

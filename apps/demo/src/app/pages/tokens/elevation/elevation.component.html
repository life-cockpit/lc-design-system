Usage Guidelines

Shadow SM (Level 1)

Use for subtle depth on cards, inputs, and small interactive elements.

Shadow MD (Level 2)

Use for raised cards, hover states, and grouped content sections.

Shadow LG (Level 3)

Use for dropdowns, popovers, and elevated interactive components.

Shadow XL (Level 4)

Use for modals, dialogs, and prominent overlays that need maximum emphasis.
